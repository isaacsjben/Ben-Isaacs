Terrorism in Scandinavia

This project is a choropleth map that I designed to illustrate a correlation between national policies on asylum and terrorism in Scandinavia. Sweden, splotched in red, stands apart from Denmark and Norway, signifying that its relaxed border policies during the European Migrant Crisis may have connections to the plague of terrorism that has largely escaped its two closely related neighbors. This map, created in ArcGIS Pro, was an exercise in emphasizing a particular narrative, albeit one that is at odds with my visual storytelling project in ArcGIS Story Maps.

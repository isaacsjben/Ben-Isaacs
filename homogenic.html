Homogenic

This project is an ode to one of my favorite works in Scandinavian music, the 1997 album Homogenic by the Icelandic songstress Bjork. Homogenic conceptualizes the untamed beauty of the Icelandic landscape in terms of sound, and I wanted the icons on this map style to catalogue the landforms that are sonically portrayed by the album. The map aesthetic itself is inspired by the album cover, which features a cyborg-like Bjork in a dress that resembles a fiery volcano covered in snow. I conveyed this aesthetic heavily through color, employing Mapbox Studio to imbue my map with pale blues, purples, and whites alongside bold reds and black.
